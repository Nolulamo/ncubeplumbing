Our Services

Bathroom Renovations

Our team of skilled plumbers and tilers can transform your bathroom into a modern, functional, and visually appealing space. From complete overhauls to minor upgrades, we've got you covered.

About Us

Our History

Ncube's Plumbing was founded in 2010 with the goal of providing high-quality plumbing and bathroom renovation services to the local community. Over the years, we've expanded our expertise to include iron gate fabrication and installation.

Our team of skilled professionals is dedicated to delivering exceptional results and exceeding our clients' expectations.

Our Team

At Ncube's Plumbing, we have a team of experienced and certified plumbers, welders, and tilers who work together to ensure the success of every project.

Our team members have years of expertise in their respective fields and are committed to providing exceptional service to our clients.
